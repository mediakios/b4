# Semantic Categories

## obyek

Entity or service being discussed.

## problem

Failure, issue, or undesired condition.

## goal

Desired future state.

## aksi

Action performed or requested.

## request

Request marker such as asking, requesting, or seeking confirmation.

## keadaan

State marker such as already, not yet, still, or currently.

## context

Additional contextual meaning not covered by other categories.
